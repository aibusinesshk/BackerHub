### CTP's Pain Points (Your Opportunities)

**1. No staking infrastructure**
CTP's 302K players have no formal way to stake each other. It all happens on LINE groups, Facebook, and handshake deals. Players get scammed. CTP's brand takes the hit when it happens at their venue.

**2. Payment friction**
Taiwanese banks don't support poker-related deposits. Players already use crypto informally. CTP has no built-in payment solution — they rely on players figuring it out themselves.

**3. Player retention beyond tournaments**
Between tournaments, CTP's relationship with players is passive. There's no engagement layer, no portfolio tracking, no investment community. Players come, play, leave.

**4. International expansion complexity**
CTP wants to grow beyond Taiwan (Korea, Vietnam, Philippines) but has no tech infrastructure for cross-border staking, identity verification, or settlement.

**5. Trust & fraud prevention**
Informal staking deals gone wrong damage CTP's reputation. They need a trust layer — but building one from scratch is expensive and not their core competency.

**6. Data & analytics gap**
CTP streams games but doesn't offer players analytics, ROI tracking, or reliability scores. Modern poker platforms (GGPoker, PokerStars) do.

## 4. The Deal Structure

### What You Offer CTP: 30% Equity

| Term | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Equity** | 30% of BackerHub (negotiable up to 35% max) |
| **Vesting** | 4-year vesting, 1-year cliff |
| **Your retention** | 65-70% — you maintain control |
| **Board seat** | CTP gets 1 board observer seat (not a controlling vote) |

### What CTP Provides in Return

| Deliverable | Value |
|-------------|-------|
| **Exclusive partnership** | BackerHub is CTP's sole official staking platform |
| **Player access** | Integration into CTP's player app / registration flow |
| **Brand endorsement** | "Powered by BackerHub" on CTP materials, streams |
| **Tournament integration** | BackerHub listed as official staking partner for TMT, APA events |
| **Monthly player flow** | Minimum referral targets (e.g., 500 new users in first 6 months) |
| **Data sharing** | Tournament schedules, player registrations fed into BackerHub |
| **International extension** | BackerHub partnership extends to CTP's future venues/events |
| **Content & streaming** | BackerHub featured on CTP Twitch/YouTube streams |

### Why 30% (Not Revenue Share)

David is a builder. He'll understand equity better than a rev-share model because:
- Rev-share feels transactional — equity feels like co-ownership
- 30% aligns CTP's incentives with BackerHub's growth
- If BackerHub reaches $5M GTV in Year 1, CTP's 30% is worth real money at Series A
- Your key line: 「你們不是投資者，你們是 co-builder」

### Vesting Protects You

The 4-year vesting with 1-year cliff means:
- If CTP does nothing for 12 months, they get 0%
- They earn 7.5% per year after the cliff
- If the partnership falls apart in Year 2, CTP only has 15%, not 30%
- This ensures CTP actually delivers players, not just lends their name

> 「你不需要。BackerHub 已經全部開發完成。CTP 只需要做你們最擅長的事：推廣給玩家、整合到賽事中。技術面全部由我負責。」

### "Why should we give our players to your platform?"

> 「你的玩家已經在 stake 了——只是在 LINE 群組裡，沒有保護、沒有驗證、沒有追索權。BackerHub 不是把玩家帶走，是把他們留在 CTP 的生態系裡，用更安全的方式做他們本來就在做的事。」

### "What if another platform approaches us?"

> 「StakeKings 是美國公司，沒有中文、沒有加密貨幣、沒有亞洲策略。PokerStake 是 GGPoker 旗下的，他們不會幫 CTP 的 live 賽事。GGPoker 的內建 staking 只支援線上。BackerHub 是唯一一個專為亞洲 live 錦標賽打造的平台，而且已經開發完成。」

### "30% is too much / too little"

Too much:
> 「30% 代表我對這個合作的誠意。我可以找投資人拿錢只給 10-15%，但我選擇給 CTP 30%，因為你們的價值不是錢可以衡量的——302,505 位玩家、全球第一的品牌、台灣撲克的合法推動者。」

Too little:
> 「我保留 65-70% 是因為我需要控制權來快速迭代產品。但 CTP 的 30% 在 Series A 時會非常值錢——如果我們達到 $5M GTV，以 10x 估值計算，CTP 的持股就值 $1.5M。而你們的投入是品牌和流量，不是現金。」

### "We need to think about it"

> 「當然，這是一個重要的決定。我可以先做一個 30 天的試點——在下一場 TMT 或 APA 賽事中讓 BackerHub 作為非官方合作，看看玩家的反應。如果數據好，我們再正式簽約。」
